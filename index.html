Activities

Bike

If you don't have your own bike, you can rent on from one of the serveral bike rental vendors in Killarney to take on our paved trails around Ross Castle and Muckross.

Hike

Killarney National Park is full of well-maintained trails for every kind of fitness level.

Paddle

Take a kayak or canoe one of three stunning lakes and to find hidden coves, strange rock formations, and visit Inisfallen Island — a 7th-century monastic settlement that was once a bastion of learning and knowledge in medieval Europe.

Boat

Try one of our relaxing cruises with some of our knowledgeable tourgides. You'll be taken around Lough Leane in comfort while a guide describes the natural and cultural history of Killarney National Park.
